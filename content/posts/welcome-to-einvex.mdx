If you've ever opened a Moroccan brokerage account and tried to figure out
which listed companies you can actually invest in with a clean conscience,
you know the answer is: **good luck**. There's no public, structured
Sharia screening for the Casablanca Stock Exchange. Investors either
guess, settle, or stay on the sidelines.

EinveX exists to fix that.

## What's live today

The current product focuses on three things:

- **Market** — a trading-style market view of every Casablanca-listed
  company, with sector filters, top movers, and a MASI-style market estimate.
- **Stock detail** — a 1-year price chart with SMA20 / SMA50, day range,
  52-week range, momentum, volatility, and the AAOIFI screening detail
  for each company.
- **Portfolio** — log your buys and sells, watch your equity curve,
  track your sector allocation, and see your dividend purification
  obligations and Zakat all in one place.

Everything is stored locally in your browser for now — no account
required. We'll add real persistence in the next milestone.

## Why we started here

When the recent IPO wave hit Casablanca, the first question we asked
ourselves before each listing wasn't "is this profitable?" It was
"is this Sharia-compliant?" And we couldn't find a clear answer
without spending hours digging through annual reports.

The four AAOIFI screening filters — activity, debt-to-market-cap,
cash-to-market-cap, and non-compliant income share — aren't hard
to understand. The data just isn't aggregated anywhere ethical
investors can rely on. We're aggregating it.

## What's next

- Real-time quotes via the BVC feed, with resilient fallbacks while the
  market-data integration matures.
- Per-ticker AAOIFI ratios populated from 2024 / 2025 filings.
- Portfolio sync across devices, once we wire up auth + a database.
- News posts for screening explainers, market commentary,
  and product updates.
